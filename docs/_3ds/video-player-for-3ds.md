You can play .mp4 file on your 3DS!

Performance
Software decoding
256x144(144p)@30fps(H.264) on OLD 3DS
640x360(360p)@24fps(H.264) on NEW 3DS
Hardware decoding (new 3ds only)
854x480(480p)@40~50fps(H.264) on NEW 3DS
