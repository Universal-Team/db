**HB Dependency Doctor** is a diagnostic tool for modded Nintendo 3DS consoles. It scans your SD card and CFW setup for common problems, misconfigurations, and leftover files, then presents a colour-coded, scrollable, filterable report with one-button auto-fixes where possible.

**9 check modules:** Luma3DS, plugins, tickets, cheats, homebrew, system, LayeredFS, sysmodules, and hardware.

**Interactive hardware test** lets you verify every button, D-pad, circle pad, and touch screen. A full report can be exported to `/HBDD_report.txt`.
