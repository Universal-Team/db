Can't decide what to play? Let your 3DS pick for you.

Scans installed titles on SD card and/or NAND, shows a readable name, and launches a random pick from your library. Great for rediscovering games you forgot you had.

**What it does:**
- Random pick from your library, with reroll before launch
- Readable title names from installed icons (SMDH), with an offline catalog as fallback
- Filter by category: apps, Virtual Console, DSiWare, demos, DLC, patches, system titles, and more
- Scan SD card and/or NAND; unlisted/homebrew mode for titles not in the catalog
- Options and filters saved to SD card
- Game, Details, and Technical info pages while browsing a pick

**Controls:**
- `A` — Launch the selected title
- `Y` — Reroll
- `L/R` — Change info page
- `X` — Toggle unlisted/homebrew mode
- `SELECT` — Options / filters
- `START` — Exit
