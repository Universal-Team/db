**Blimp Chicken** is a 3D arcade flight and combat game for the Nintendo DS.

You have taken the company blimp for a joyride with a cooler full of drinks, and the Air Force (and nature itself) are not amused. Fly a lumbering airship through a warzone of fighter jets, birds, and skyscrapers.

## Features

- **Survival mode:** last as long as you can as jets, birds, and the difficulty ramp close in.
- **Destructible city:** ram skyscrapers to wreck them for points, or fly straight through enemy jets to down them.
- **Drink and smoke:** hold a beer to your mouth to heal (at the cost of a woozy screen wobble), or light a cigarette for a temporary speed boost.
- **Local wireless VS:** up to 8 players in a last-blimp-standing dogfight. Every console runs the ROM (needs real hardware).
- **Emblem editor:** paint a custom 32x32 design with the stylus, wrapped onto both sides of your blimp.
- **Original soundtrack and SFX**, an animated opening crawl, and hull-color customization.

## Controls

- **D-pad** (or Y/A/X/B): steer and float
- **L / R:** throttle
- **Touch screen:** drag from the center and flick to throw a drink or cigarette
- **START:** pause

Direct-boots on any DS, DSi, or 3DS via a flashcard or TWiLight Menu++. No BIOS needed.
