  information tool for the Nintendo DS &amp; DSi.</p>

  <p dir="auto">An ASCII DS Lite / DSi clamshell logo on the top screen, with a column
  of real system info on the bottom screen: hardware model, running mode, ARM9/ARM7
  clocks, RAM, language, favorite color, birthday, battery, RTC, SD-card storage,
  MAC address, and — in DSi mode — region and serial number. Anything that can''t
  be read on the hardware is simply omitted.</p>

  <h3 dir="auto">Install</h3>

  <p dir="auto">Copy <code class="notranslate">DSFetch.nds</code> anywhere on your
  SD card <strong>except</strong> the <code class="notranslate">/_nds/</code> folder
  (e.g. a <code class="notranslate">roms/nds/</code> folder) and launch it from <a
  href="https://github.com/DS-Homebrew/TWiLightMenu">TWiLight Menu++</a> or any DS
  homebrew launcher.</p>
